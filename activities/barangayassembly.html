Barangay Assembly

The **Barangay Assembly of Brgy. Ilosong, Plaridel** is a significant event that brings together the leadership of both the **Sangguniang Kabataan (SK)** and the **Sangguniang Barangay (SB)** to discuss, plan, and implement programs aimed at improving the welfare of the community. Held regularly, this assembly fosters collaboration between the youth sector and the local government, ensuring that both the concerns of the young people and the general public are addressed. During the assembly, the SK and SB leaders, along with barangay officials, engage with residents, providing updates on current projects, listening to community concerns, and discussing future initiatives. This open dialogue allows for a transparent, participatory approach to governance, where the voices of all sectors are heard. It’s a celebration of unity, cooperation, and community empowerment, as both the youth and the elders work hand-in-hand for the development of Brgy. Ilosong. The assembly also serves as a platform for community members to propose new ideas, share concerns, and learn about ongoing projects that directly impact their lives. Whether it’s about youth programs, infrastructure improvements, or social services, the Brgy. Ilosong Barangay Assembly is a vital event in shaping the future of the community. It’s an embodiment of teamwork, with the SK and SB working side by side to build a stronger, more inclusive barangay.
