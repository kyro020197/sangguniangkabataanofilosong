Brigada Eskwela (August 5, 2024)

The Brigada Eskwela 2024 at Ilosong Elementary School, volunteers, supported by the Department of Education (DepEd), Sangguniang Kabataan (SK), and local organizations, work together to prepare classrooms for the school year. With the theme "Bayanihan para sa Matatag na Paaralan", it emphasizes the Filipino value of bayanihan (community unity) in creating a clean and conducive learning environment.It showcases teamwork, classroom cleaning, and shared responsibility, reflecting the spirit of collaboration and dedication to education.
