Showcasing the vibrant activities and events by SK members in our community.

Singing Contest (May 13, 2024)

The Singing Contest of Brgy. Ilosong, Plaridel, Quezon is an exciting and vibrant celebration of local talent, where participants from the community come together to showcase their musical abilities. Held annually as part of the barangay's festivities, this event attracts a wide range of singers, from aspiring youth to seasoned performers. The contest offers a platform for individuals to demonstrate their vocal prowess and passion for music, often featuring a mix of traditional Filipino songs, modern hits, and soulful ballads. The event takes place in a festive atmosphere, with a stage set up in the heart of the barangay, surrounded by cheering locals, family members, and friends who eagerly support the contestants. Contestants are judged on their vocal performance, stage presence, and interpretation of the song, with local judges or musical experts chosen to ensure a fair and exciting competition. The Singing Contest is more than just a contest—it is a community gathering that fosters camaraderie, pride, and appreciation for local culture. It encourages creative expression and helps bring the people of Brgy. Ilosong closer together, all while celebrating the rich musical heritage of Plaridel, Quezon. Whether you're a participant or a spectator, the event is sure to be a memorable experience filled with joy, excitement, and community spirit.

palarong lahi

The **Palarong Lahi** during the **Fiesta of San Isidro Labrador** in **Brgy. Ilosong** is a vibrant and exciting event that brings the community together through traditional Filipino games and friendly competition. Organized by the **Sangguniang Kabataan (SK)** of Brgy. Ilosong, the event is a highlight of the annual fiesta celebration, reflecting the rich cultural heritage and communal spirit of the barangay. The **Palarong Lahi**, or traditional Filipino games, includes a variety of fun and engaging activities that encourage teamwork, physical skill, and a sense of camaraderie. Games such as **sack race**, **tug of war**, **palosebo**, and **patintero** are enjoyed by people of all ages. The event is designed to celebrate the Filipino values of bayanihan (community spirit) and friendly competition, with the SK leading the charge to revive and preserve these traditional games for younger generations. Held in honor of **San Isidro Labrador**, the patron saint of farmers, the fiesta is an opportunity for the people of Brgy. Ilosong to express gratitude for their blessings and celebrate their local culture. With the SK taking the initiative, the Palarong Lahi serves as a way to bring people closer, create lasting memories, and inject a fun and energetic atmosphere into the festivities. Whether participating in the games or cheering on the teams, everyone enjoys the lively spirit of this unique event. The **Palarong Lahi** is more than just a competition—it's a celebration of community, culture, and the Filipino way of life, where every laugh, cheer, and victory strengthens the bond within Brgy. Ilosong.
