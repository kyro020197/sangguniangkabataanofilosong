Sangguniang Kabataan Activities

Showcasing the vibrant activities and events by SK members in our community.

Kalinisan Day (February 10, 2024)

Kalinisan Day in Brgy. Ilosong is a community event focused on cleaning and taking care of the environment. Residents come together to clean streets, parks, and public spaces, helping to keep the barangay clean and safe. The day includes clean-up activities, where everyone, young and old, participates with brooms, gloves, and trash bags. There are also short talks about waste management and how to live more sustainably. The event promotes unity and environmental awareness, reminding everyone of their role in keeping the community clean..

Kalinisan Day (March 03, 2024)

Kalinisan Day in Brgy. Ilosong is a day dedicated to cleaning and improving the barangay. On this day, residents join forces to tidy up public areas, including streets, parks, and other shared spaces. Everyone, from children to adults, helps by picking up trash, sweeping, and organizing the surroundings. There are also educational activities about proper waste disposal and the importance of a clean environment. Kalinisan Day fosters community spirit and encourages everyone to care for their surroundings and work together for a cleaner, greener Brgy. Ilosong.
